## ingredients 

- six eggs
- one or two grab of shrimp
- half onion

## recipe
 
1. quad cut onions
2. stir eggs and add salt
(pan is in mid to high heat)
3. fry eggs with oil 
  - oil is bit more than usual
  - softness as a omlet
2. remove eggs from pan
4. light fry onions (high heat)
5. add shrimp to pan
6. return eggs in the pan
